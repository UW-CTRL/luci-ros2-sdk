# Equipment Operating Guide

This guide covers the operation and maintenance of the **Sunrise Medical Quickie (Q400 M / Q500 M) Battery System** and the **Portable Power Station**.

---

## 1. Quickie Wheelchair Battery Care (24V System)
To ensure maximum range and battery longevity, follow these manufacturer guidelines.

### Battery Care Plan
* **Daily Charging:** Charge the batteries every night, regardless of how much they were used.
* **Keep Connected:** If the mobility device is not required for use, it should remain connected to the charger until required. This will not damage the batteries.
* **Avoid "Topping Up":** Do not "top up" the charge during the day. Wait until the evening for a full overnight charge.
* **Complete Cycles:** Do not interrupt the charging cycle once it has started.
* **Long-Term Storage:** If the vehicle will not be used for more than a week, charge the batteries fully and then disconnect the main battery lead.
* **Battery Type:** Only use 12V Gel or Lead Acid batteries connected in series (24V).

### Charging Procedure
1.  **Power Down:** Switch off the wheelchair controller.
2.  **Connect to Joystick:** Plug the charger's round output plug into the charging socket located on the front of the joystick module.
3.  **Wall Power:** Plug the charger into the wall outlet and switch it on.
4.  **LED Check:** The charger LED will indicate the status (refer to the charger casing for specific light codes).
5.  **Duration:** Charging can take up to **12 hours** depending on depletion.

### Critical Safety
* **Temperature:** Never charge a frozen battery. If it is below -9°C (16°F), let it thaw completely first.
* **Placement:** Always place the charger on a **hard, flat surface**. Never place it on carpet, soft upholstery, or directly on top of the battery.
* **Ventilation:** The charger casing gets hot during normal operation; ensure it is not covered.

## 2. 8A Off-Board Charger Specifications (Model 4C24080A)
* **Input:** 100-240V AC, 50/60Hz, 3.8A max.
* **Output:** 24V DC @ 8A.
* **Charging Voltage:** 28.8V ± 0.2V.
* **Floating Voltage:** 27.6V ± 0.2V.
* **Protections:** Short Circuit, Reverse Power, Overheat, and 12-hour Automatic Cut-off.
* **Environment:** Operating temperature -25°C to 40°C; Humidity 20% to 85%.

---

## 3. Portable Power Station (Lithium-Ion)
Used for charging mobile devices and small AC electronics.

### Technical Specs
* **Capacity:** 88.8Wh (6Ah/14.8V).
* **AC Output:** 110V/60Hz, 80W Continuous (120W Max). *Modified Sinewave.*
* **USB Outputs:** 2x USB 5V-2.4A, 2x USB QC 3.0 (18W Max), 1x USB-C PD 2.0 (18W Max).

### How to Charge the Unit
1.  **AC Wall Charger:** Plug the included AC adapter into the DC input (19V/1.5A).
2.  **Solar Charging:** Supports DC 15V-24V / 1.2A Max.
3.  **Indicators:** Blue LED indicators blink during charging and remain solid when fully charged (approx. 5-6 hours).

### LED Light Panel Operation
* **Single Press:** Flashlight ON.
* **Second Press:** Higher Brightness.
* **Third Press:** SOS Mode (Blinking).
* **Fourth Press:** Flashlight OFF.

---

## Safety & Maintenance Summary
* **No Disassembly:** Do not attempt to repair or open the charger or power station casings.
* **Moisture:** These units are for indoor use or dry conditions only (IP21 rating for the 8A charger).
* **Cable Safety:** Ensure cords are positioned where they won't be stepped on, tripped over, or stressed.
